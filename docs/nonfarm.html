Job Change Comparison by State

These charts show the change in total nonfarm employment from the beginning of COVID (February, 2020) and the start of the second Trump Administration (January, 2025). Change is calculated using the most recent month of data available (currently: December 2025). Blue bars represent state data, red bars provide a comparison with national data.
